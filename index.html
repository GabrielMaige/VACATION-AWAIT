About Us

Your Pathway to Success in Tourism & Hospitality

Vacation Awaits Utalii College is a fully registered institution located in Mbauda Arusha, Tanzania. We are committed to providing high-quality Certificate courses in various fields of tourism, hospitality, and language studies.

Our institution is registered with BRELA (Business Registrations and Licensing Agency) and The Institute of Adults Education. We offer comprehensive Certificate courses designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge for successful careers.
